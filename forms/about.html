Know more About us

The horrors of the second wave of corona virus infection in India are too painful to forget. Thousands, if not lakhs, of people died not just as a result of the virus, but also due to a lack of crucial medical supplies and consumables. We witnessed the heinous practise of black marketing medical oxygen cylinders and antiviral medications, Remdesivir and Tocilizumab, on a large scale. Some consumers spent more than a lakh rupee for a vial of Remedesivir, which has a maximum price of roughly 5000 rupees.

Taking into account all of these aspects, we developed this online pharmacy, which is far superior to the online pharmacies available today.

Our website will make it simple for you to find medical supplies. Our website relies on its users to help other patients in need. It has a particular function that will alert us to immediately supply the required medical supply; and alert our users ( in case our sourcing of that medicine takes longer than normal) to help us to locate life-saving consumables, medical supplies, hospital beds, and ambulance services for the needy on time.

How Our Website Works?

Let's take a closer look at this. A man named John requires a medication known as X, but he cannot locate it; even our website is out of stock of this medication. However, John's need is pressing. So, what will John do in this case?

He'll find our website. Send his request to our chat bot. Our chat bot will question every relevant user of our website about the availability of the drug, and if someone discovers the medicine before us, we will notify John of the location of the store where the medicine is accessible. If the shop is in another part of India, we will use our network to locate the drug and deliver it to John. Now, John is content since he has received the medications he need, but what about our user who went above and above to locate those medications? We will recognize his efforts by awarding him "Lifesaver" points, which he may redeem on his next medical supplies purchase from us.
